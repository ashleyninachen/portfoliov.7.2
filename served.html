AR/UI Mobile Design   •   Mockups and Wireframes  

TEAM: Ashley Chen (Designer), CEO X, Developer X



Context

Serv'd is a concept app that arose from a contemporary desire- to know what your food looks like in 3D before you order it. Serv'd is a team in UC Irvine's highly coveted Wayfinder Incubator; after their original designer- who initially conducted the UX research- left, I joined the team as their co-founder to translate the research and prior data to design a mobile application with AR capabilities.

My designs for Serv'd are currently under NDA, so please reach out to me for more deets! You're welcome to check out the test demos of the concept app below.
